Quora World Meetup at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Benagaluru

QuoRa is the abbrevation for Quora Ramaiah, which is a group of all the Quorans at Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT), Bengaluru. QuoRa is a closely knit circle of Quorans at RIT, who meet weekly, interact with one another and share their knowledge and experiences.

We are happy to announce our first QuoRa World Meetup- the Quora World Meetup 2017 at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, which is scheduled on November 10th, 2017(Friday). This QuoRa Meetup is one of the many officially recognized meetups that are going to be organized as a part of Quora World Meetup Week 2017. (View Here). The meetup will tentatively be a 3 hours affair starting at 3:00 PM(GST+5:30).

QuoRa is happy to announce its first ever QuoRa World Meetup at RIT, Bengaluru on 10th Novemeber, 2017. All the Quorans across Bengaluru and the globe are duly invited. Feel free to gate crash at our QuoRa Meetup and enlighten the fellow Quorans with your knowledge and tales of wisdom.

We, however, recommend all the Quorans, who are planning to pay us a visit, to kindly fill out the registration form to help us organize things better.
